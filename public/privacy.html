Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This privacy policy describes how the Our Kitchen Alexa skill and companion web application collect, use, and protect your information.

Developer

Our Kitchen is developed by Nick M.

Information We Collect

When you use Our Kitchen, the following data may be collected and stored:

  • Device identifier — used to link your Alexa device to your household
  • Voice PIN — used for authentication when linking a device
  • Meal planning data — meals, weekly meal assignments, and categories
  • Recipes — recipe names, ingredients, and instructions
  • Grocery lists — grocery items, quantities, and store assignments
  • Household items — recurring products tracked for restocking

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Our Kitchen service:

  • Meal planning and weekly meal browsing
  • Recipe access and step-by-step cooking guidance via voice
  • Grocery list management (adding, removing, and organizing items by store)
  • Household item tracking for quick restocking

Data Storage

All data is stored securely using Google Firebase and Cloud Firestore. Device linking information is stored in Amazon DynamoDB via the Alexa Skills Kit persistence adapter.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal data to any third parties. Your data is only accessed by the Our Kitchen skill and web application to provide the service.

Data Deletion

You can manage or delete your data in the following ways:

  • Unlink your device — say "Alexa, tell our kitchen to unlink this device" to remove the device-household association
  • Manage data via the web app — use the Our Kitchen web application to edit or delete meals, recipes, grocery items, and household items
  • Contact us — email us to request full data deletion

Children's Privacy

Our Kitchen is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
