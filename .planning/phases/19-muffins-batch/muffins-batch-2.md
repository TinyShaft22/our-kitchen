## Recipe 12: Apple Crisp Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/apple-crisp-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** Not specified
**Cook Time:** 20-25 minutes

### Ingredients - Muffins
- 1 medium apple
- 3/4 cup light brown sugar
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 1/4 cup vegetable oil
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Ingredients - Crisp Topping
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup old-fashioned oatmeal
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- Pinch of nutmeg
- 4 tablespoons butter, chilled

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line 12 muffin cups with liners.
2. Peel and grate one medium apple using a large-holed grater.
3. Combine brown sugar, eggs, oil, and vanilla in a bowl. Fold in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt, then fold in grated apples.
4. Mix crumble ingredients separately. Use a pastry cutter to work butter through until the mixture resembles coarse sand.
5. Distribute batter evenly among 12 muffin cups and top with crumble mixture.
6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, then cool slightly before serving.

## Recipe 13: Apple Crumble Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/apple-crumble-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

### Ingredients - Muffins
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup light brown sugar
- 2 eggs, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 6 tablespoons Greek yogurt
- 6 tablespoons milk
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 apple, peeled and chopped

### Ingredients - Streusel Topping
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup light brown sugar
- 2/3 cup all-purpose flour

### Ingredients - Vanilla Glaze (Optional)
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Prepare 12 muffin cups with liners.
2. Combine streusel ingredients using a fork or pastry cutter until the mixture resembles wet sand.
3. Mix melted butter and sugars together. Add eggs one at a time, then Greek yogurt, milk, and vanilla.
4. Add flour, baking powder, spices, and salt; mix until partially combined. Fold in apple pieces.
5. Fill muffin cups three-quarters full using a large cookie scoop or 1/4 cup measure.
6. Top each muffin with approximately 1 tablespoon streusel, pressing gently into batter.
7. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then reduce to 375°F and bake 15 additional minutes until golden.
8. While baking, combine glaze ingredients if desired.
9. Cool completely before drizzling with icing.

## Recipe 14: Apple Carrot Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/apple-carrot-muffins/

**Yield:** 10 muffins
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 35 minutes

### Ingredients - Muffins
- 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 3/4 cup buttermilk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 cup apples, peeled and chopped
- 2/3 cup shredded carrots
- 1/3 cup shredded coconut
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt

### Ingredients - Streusel Topping
- 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons brown sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon
- Pinch of salt
- 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, softened

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a muffin tin with 10 cups and spray with oil-based spray.
2. Combine brown sugar, eggs, melted butter, buttermilk, and vanilla in a large bowl.
3. Stir in apples, carrots, and coconut until blended.
4. In a separate bowl, mix flour, baking powder, and salt.
5. Fold dry ingredients into wet mixture until just combined—lumps are acceptable.
6. Make streusel: combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, and salt; cut in butter with a fork until resembling wet sand.
7. Fill muffin cups nearly to the top; sprinkle with streusel.
8. Bake 5 minutes at 425°F, then reduce to 350°F and bake 15 additional minutes (don't open oven door).
9. Cool 10 minutes in tin, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.

## Recipe 15: Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/pumpkin-chocolate-chip-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 35 minutes

### Ingredients
- 3/4 cup pumpkin puree
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 3/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

### Instructions

4. Fill muffin cups just over two-thirds full. Sprinkle with finishing sugar if desired.
5. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then reduce to 375°F and bake 15 additional minutes.
6. Cool slightly before serving.

## Recipe 16: Homemade Raisin Bran Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/not-your-grandmas-bran-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 25-30 minutes

### Ingredients
- 2 cups wheat bran
- 1 1/2 cups dark raisins (divided: 1 cup + 1/2 cup)
- 1 1/2 cups water (divided: 1 cup + 1/2 cup)
- 1/2 cup buttermilk or plain low- or non-fat yogurt
- Fresh orange zest (a few swipes)
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 2 large eggs
- 3/4 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line a 12-cup muffin tin with paper liners.
2. Toast wheat bran on a baking sheet for 6-8 minutes, stirring occasionally. Cool completely.
3. Heat 1 cup raisins with 1/2 cup water, simmer 10 minutes until absorbed. Puree until smooth.
4. Combine toasted bran, buttermilk/yogurt, 1 cup water, raisin puree, orange zest, and brown sugar in a large bowl. Stir in oil and eggs.
5. Mix flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Sift into wet ingredients and stir until just combined. Fold in remaining 1/2 cup raisins.
6. Spoon batter into muffin tins, mounding slightly.
7. Bake 25-30 minutes until centers feel set.

## Recipe 17: Morning Glory Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/the-best-morning-glory-muffins/

**Yield:** 24 muffins
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 17 minutes

### Ingredients
- 3/4 cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup brown sugar, packed
- 3 eggs, room temperature
- 2 cups shredded carrots, packed
- 1 cup shredded apples
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/4 cup orange juice
- 1 1/4 cup whole wheat flour
- 1 cup oat flour
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ginger
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1/2 cup raisins
- 1/2 cup unsweetened shredded coconut (optional)

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line 24 muffin cups with liners and grease the tin tops.
2. Whisk together oil, brown sugar, and eggs. Fold in carrots, apples, vanilla, and orange juice.
3. Add flours, baking soda, baking powder, salt, and spices to wet ingredients. Mix gently without overmixing. Fold in raisins and coconut.
4. Scoop 1/3 cup batter into each muffin cup.
5. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then reduce to 375°F for 12 additional minutes until springy and a knife inserted comes out mostly clean.
6. Cool before serving.

**Note:** This recipe uses whole wheat and oat flour for a heartier, healthier muffin.

## Recipe 18: Healthy Peanut Butter Banana Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/healthy-peanut-butter-banana-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes

### Ingredients
- 3 large ripe bananas
- 1/2 cup natural peanut butter
- 4 tablespoons honey
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups oat flour*
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup dark chocolate chips

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Line a muffin tin with 12 cups and spray with oil-based spray. Set aside.
2. In a large bowl, combine bananas, peanut butter, honey, eggs, and vanilla. Stir until well mixed. Add oat flour, baking powder, and salt; fold to combine. Add chocolate chips and avoid overmixing.
3. Scoop batter into prepared cups, filling just below the rim.
4. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then—without opening the oven—reduce temperature to 350°F and bake 15 additional minutes.
5. Remove from oven and cool for 10 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.

**Note:** Gluten-free and refined sugar-free. Make oat flour by blending regular oats in a food processor.

## Recipe 19: Pumpkin Carrot Cake Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/pumpkin-carrot-cake-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 40 minutes

### Ingredients - Muffin Batter
- 3/4 cup freshly shredded carrots
- 1/2 cup pumpkin puree
- 1/2 cup sweetened, shredded coconut
- 1/4 cup raisins
- 3/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/3 cup vegetable oil
- 2 eggs, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Ingredients - Glaze
- 1 tablespoon maple syrup
- 3 tablespoons milk
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- Pinch of salt
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F and line 12 muffin cups with cupcake liners.
2. Mix carrots, pumpkin, coconut, raisins, sugar, oil, eggs, and vanilla in a large bowl.
3. Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves, and salt in a separate bowl.
4. Fold dry ingredients into wet mixture until just combined.
5. Divide batter evenly among 12 muffin tins using a 1/3 cup measure or large ice cream scoop.
6. Bake at 425°F for 5 minutes, then reduce to 375°F and bake 15 minutes more until a knife inserted in the center comes out mostly clean.
7. Cool muffins slightly. While cooling, whisk together all glaze ingredients until smooth.
8. Drizzle glaze over warm muffins and serve.

## Recipe 20: Carrot Cake Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/carrot-cake-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 muffins
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes
**Total Time:** 35 minutes

### Ingredients - Muffins
- 1/2 cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 1/2 cup plain Greek yogurt
- 1/2 cup milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cups shredded carrots
- 1/3 cup shredded coconut
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon baking powder
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1/2 cup raisins (optional)

### Ingredients - Glaze
- 1 1/3 cups powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- 1/4 cup walnuts, finely chopped

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 425°F. Grease 12 muffin tins and line with cupcake liners.
2. Combine oil, brown sugar, eggs, yogurt, milk, vanilla, carrots, and coconut in a large bowl; whisk until combined.
3. Add flour, baking powder, salt, and cinnamon to wet ingredients; whisk until just combined. Fold in raisins if using.
4. Fill muffin tins about 3/4 full using a large cookie scoop or 1/4 cup measure.
5. Bake 5 minutes at 425°F, then reduce to 375°F and bake 15 minutes more until golden brown.
6. Prepare glaze by mixing powdered sugar, milk, vanilla, and salt until smooth.
7. Cool muffins completely. Microwave glaze 30 seconds to thin, then spoon over each muffin. Top with walnuts if desired.

## Recipe 21: Cinnamon Roll Muffins

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/cinnamon-roll-muffins/

**Yield:** 12 rolls
**Prep Time:** 2 hours 30 minutes (includes rise time)
**Cook Time:** 20 minutes

### Ingredients - Dough
- 4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1 packet (2 1/4 tsp.) Rapid Rise Instant Yeast
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups milk
- 6 tablespoons butter
- 1 egg, room temperature

### Ingredients - Filling
- 1/4 cup butter, room temperature
- 2/3 cup light brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
- Pinch of salt

### Ingredients - Glaze
- 1 tablespoon cream cheese
- 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
- 2-3 tablespoons milk
- Pinch salt
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Instructions
1. Combine 2 cups flour, yeast, salt, and sugar. Mix together.
2. Warm milk and butter in microwave for 30 seconds. Add egg, milk, and butter to flour mixture.
3. Beat on low speed, gradually increasing to high for 2 minutes until combined.
4. Add remaining flour gradually until dough pulls away from bowl. Knead 10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
5. Transfer to oiled bowl, cover, and rise 1 hour until doubled.
6. Mix filling ingredients separately.
7. Roll dough into 18x12 inch rectangle. Spread butter and sprinkle cinnamon mixture over it.
8. Roll tightly from long end and pinch edges closed.
9. Cut into 12 pieces using floss or sharp knife. Place in buttered muffin tin.
10. Cover and rise 1 hour until doubled and puffy.
11. Bake at 350°F for 20 minutes until golden brown.
12. Prepare glaze by microwaving cream cheese 15-30 seconds, then mixing with remaining ingredients.
13. Spoon glaze over cooled rolls and serve warm.

**Note:** This is a yeasted muffin recipe requiring rise time. Different from quick muffins.
