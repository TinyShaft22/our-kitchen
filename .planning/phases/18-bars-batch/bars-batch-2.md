## 13. Peppermint Bark Rice Krispie Treats

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/peppermint-bark-rice-krispie-treats/

**Yield:** 16 bars
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes
**Chill Time:** 30 minutes
**Total Time:** 50 minutes

### Ingredients
- 6 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 12 ounces marshmallows (about 6 1/2 cups)
- 5 1/2 cups Rice Krispie cereal
- 1/2 cup crushed peppermint candies, divided
- Pinch of salt
- 8 ounces dark or bittersweet chocolate, chopped
- 4 ounces white chocolate, chopped
- 1 tablespoon coconut oil

### Instructions
1. Line 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper; grease with cooking spray.
2. Melt butter over medium-low heat; add marshmallows, stirring until melted (3-5 minutes). Remove from heat; fold in cereal until coated.
3. Add most crushed peppermints (reserve some for topping); fold until combined. Transfer to pan; press gently into even layer. Refrigerate.
4. Microwave dark chocolate with coconut oil on high in 30-second intervals until melted.
5. Microwave white chocolate on high in 15-second intervals until melted.
6. Top bars with dark chocolate spread evenly. Add white chocolate dollops (6-8) and create swirls with toothpick. Top with remaining peppermints. Refrigerate 30 minutes.
7. Cut into squares and serve.

**Notes:** Store in airtight container in refrigerator for up to 2 weeks.

## 14. Bourbon Chocolate Pecan Pie Bars

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/bourbon-chocolate-pecan-pie-bars/

**Yield:** 16 bars
**Prep Time:** 2 hours 20 minutes
**Cook Time:** 50 minutes
**Total Time:** 3 hours

### Ingredients

**For the Crust:**
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 2 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

**For the Pecan Pie Filling:**
- 3/4 cup honey
- 3/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons bourbon (optional)
- 2 1/2 cups chopped pecans (roasted & salted preferred)
- 4 ounces bittersweet chocolate, roughly chopped

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper and set aside.
2. Combine flour, brown sugar, butter, cream cheese, vanilla, and salt in stand mixer fitted with paddle attachment. Beat on low speed, gradually increasing to medium until mixture forms clumps. Press into pan and bake 10 minutes until lightly golden.
3. While crust bakes, whisk together honey, brown sugar, eggs, melted butter, salt, vanilla extract, and bourbon in a bowl.
4. Chop chocolate and pecans; toss together in a large bowl.
5. Remove crust from oven, top with pecan-chocolate mixture, then pour sugar mixture evenly over top.
6. Bake 30 minutes, then cover with foil and bake additional 10 minutes.
7. Cool at least 2 hours in refrigerator before cutting and serving.

**Notes:** Can be made up to three days ahead. Honey replaces traditional corn syrup.

## 15. Key Lime Pie Cheesecake Bars

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/key-lime-pie-cheesecake-bars/

**Yield:** 16 servings
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes
**Cook Time:** 35-40 minutes
**Total Time:** 2 hours 30 minutes

### Ingredients

**Graham Cracker Crust:**
- 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs (about 12 full sheets)
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 5 tablespoons granulated sugar
- Pinch salt

**Cheesecake Layer:**
- 2 (8-ounce) packages full fat cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons key lime zest
- 1/4 cup key lime juice
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 cup sour cream, room temperature
- 2 large eggs, room temperature

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325°F; line 9x9-inch pan with parchment paper.
2. Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and salt until combined. Press firmly into prepared pan.
3. In stand mixer with paddle attachment, beat softened cream cheese, lime zest, and sugar for 2 minutes until smooth.
4. Add lime juice, vanilla, and sour cream; mix until combined. Add eggs one at a time, beating just until combined.
5. Pour batter over crust. Drop pan on counter several times to remove air bubbles.
6. Bake 35-40 minutes on middle rack, rotating halfway through. Center will be slightly wobbly.
7. Turn off oven; open door slightly. Leave cheesecake inside for 30 minutes to cool gradually.
8. Remove and cool on rack for 1 hour, then refrigerate until ready to serve.
9. Slice into 16 squares. Top with whipped cream and lime zest if desired.

## 16. Ted Lasso Biscuits

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/ted-lasso-biscuits/

**Yield:** 1 pan (8x8 inches)
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 40 minutes
**Total Time:** 55 minutes

### Ingredients
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 2/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into cubes
- Coarse sugar for topping (optional)

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment on all sides.
2. Combine flour, sugar, salt, vanilla, almond extract, and butter in a food processor. Pulse until the mixture forms a dough around the processor blades.
3. Press the dough into the prepared pan, spreading into an even layer with your hands.
4. Sprinkle the tops generously with coarse sugar.
5. Bake at 350°F for 35-40 minutes until light golden brown. Cool completely before slicing.

**Notes:** These buttery shortbread bars stay tender and soft for up to 3 weeks when stored in a sealed container.

## 17. White Chocolate Funfetti Rice Krispie Treats

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/funfetti-rice-krispie-treats/

**Yield:** 16 servings
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Chill Time:** 50 minutes
**Total Time:** 1 hour

### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- 16 ounces marshmallows
- 6 cups rice krispies cereal
- 1/3 cup rainbow sprinkles, plus more for topping
- 1 cup white chocolate chips
- Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Line a 9x9 pan with parchment paper and grease with cooking spray
2. Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat; add marshmallows and stir until fully melted, then remove from heat
3. Pour marshmallow mixture into a large bowl with rice krispies cereal; fold together until evenly coated
4. Gently fold in rainbow sprinkles and white chocolate chips
5. Transfer mixture to prepared pan and spread into an even layer using a greased spatula
6. Optional: top with additional white chocolate chips and sprinkles
7. Refrigerate until set, then cut into 16 squares

**Notes:** Don't overcook marshmallows; work quickly as mixture hardens fast.

## 18. Peanut Butter and Fluff Rice Krispie Treats

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/peanut-butter-and-fluff-rice-krispie-treats/

**Yield:** 12 servings
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Chill Time:** 1 hour
**Total Time:** 1 hour 15 minutes

### Ingredients
- 16 oz marshmallows (1 standard bag or ~8 cups mini marshmallows)
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- Pinch of salt
- 6 cups Rice Krispies Cereal
- 1/3 cup creamy peanut butter, melted
- 1/3 cup marshmallow fluff

### Instructions
1. Line a 9x9 pan with parchment paper and spray with cooking spray
2. Melt butter in large pot over medium heat; add marshmallows and salt; stir until fully melted; remove from heat
3. Fold in Rice Krispies to fully coat cereal
4. Microwave peanut butter for 15 seconds until slightly melted
5. Drop melted peanut butter and fluff into batter; fold a few times to create thick swirls (don't fully mix)
6. Spoon mixture into prepared pan; press into even layer using cooking spray-coated spatula
7. Cool at least 1 hour before slicing into twelve treats

**Notes:** Use firm peanut butter brands like Jif or Skippy rather than natural varieties.

## 19. Cranberry Orange Shortbread

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/cranberry-orange-shortbread/

**Yield:** 16 bars
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes
**Cook Time:** 25 minutes
**Total Time:** 35 minutes

### Ingredients
- 10 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cubed
- 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons fresh orange zest
- 1/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries
- 4 ounces white chocolate, melted

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line an 8x8-inch pan with parchment paper on all sides.
2. In a food processor, combine flour, sugar, salt, orange zest, and butter. Pulse until just combined and mixture forms a dough ball.
3. Remove from processor and stir in cranberries.
4. Press dough into prepared pan, spreading into an even layer with your hands.
5. Bake at 350°F for 20-30 minutes until light golden brown. Allow bars to cool completely.
6. Drizzle cooled bars with melted white chocolate using a spoon. Refrigerate for 30 minutes or freeze briefly to set. Cut into rectangles with a sharp knife.

**Notes:** Soft, buttery, and tender treats that keep for weeks.

## 20. No Bake Pecan Pie Bars

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/no-bake-pecan-pie-bars/

**Yield:** 16 servings
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Chill Time:** 1 hour
**Total Time:** 1 hour 15 minutes

### Ingredients

**For the Crust:**
- 20 pecan shortbread cookies
- 5 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- Pinch of salt

**For the Pecan Filling:**
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup light corn syrup
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 cups pecan pieces
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Line an 8x8 square pan with parchment paper on all sides and set aside.
2. Combine pecan shortbread cookies, melted butter, and salt in a food processor; pulse until clumped together. Press mixture into prepared pan using a flat-bottomed measuring cup to create an even layer. Refrigerate to set.
3. While crust sets, combine brown sugar and corn syrup in a medium heavy-bottomed saucepan. Cook over medium-high heat until boiling. Allow mixture to boil for 30 seconds before removing from heat. Stir in vanilla extract, salt, and pecan pieces; fold to coat pecans thoroughly.
4. Quickly pour mixture over prepared crust, spreading evenly with a rubber spatula.
5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour or overnight. Slice with a sharp knife before serving.

**Notes:** Great for Thanksgiving since they require no oven space. Can be doubled in a 9x13 pan.

## 21. Cheesecake Swirled Pumpkin Bars

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/cheesecake-swirled-pumpkin-bars/

**Yield:** 16 servings
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes
**Cook Time:** 25 minutes
**Total Time:** 40 minutes

### Ingredients

**For the pumpkin bars:**
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter
- 1/2 cup brown sugar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/3 cup pure pumpkin puree
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 egg yolk
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

**For the cream cheese swirl:**
- 6 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
- 1 egg, room temperature
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- Pinch salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F; line 8x8 pan with parchment paper
2. Combine butter and sugars; microwave 30 seconds, stir, repeat twice until glossy
3. Add pumpkin puree and vanilla; mix well, then add egg yolk
4. Fold in flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until combined
5. Transfer batter to prepared pan in even layer
6. Mix cream cheese filling ingredients until smooth and lump-free
7. Drop cream cheese by large spoonfuls onto batter; swirl with butter knife (avoid over-swirling)
8. Bake 25 minutes until edges set and center puffs slightly but remains gooey
9. Cool before slicing into 16 bars

**Notes:** Don't over-swirl the batter—you want big, distinct swirls.

## 22. Salted Caramel Scotcheroos

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/salted-caramel-scotcheroos/

**Yield:** 20 squares (2-inch)
**Prep Time:** 1 hour
**Cook Time:** 30 minutes
**Total Time:** 1 hour 30 minutes

### Ingredients

**For the Salted Caramel:**
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 6 tablespoons salted butter
- 1/2 cup heavy cream
- 1 teaspoon salt

**For the Scotcheroos:**
- 1 cup creamy peanut butter
- 6 cups rice krispies
- 4 cups semi-sweet chocolate chips

### Instructions
1. Grease a 9x13 baking pan with butter, then line with parchment paper. Set aside.
2. In a small saucepan, heat sugar on low until completely melted, swirling gently every 20 seconds. This takes 10-15 minutes. Remove from heat and stir in butter (mixture will bubble vigorously). Add heavy cream and sea salt, stirring until combined. Whisk in peanut butter.
3. Pour rice krispies into a large mixing bowl. Add salted caramel peanut butter mixture and fold gently to combine.
4. Place mixture into prepared pan, pressing evenly. Set aside.
5. Heat chocolate chips in a double boiler over low heat until fully melted. Pour over krispies, smoothing to edges.
6. Allow to cool completely (about 1 hour) before cutting into 2-inch squares.

**Notes:** Balances sweetness with homemade salted caramel rather than butterscotch.

## 23. Baklava Cheesecake Bars

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/baklava-cheesecake-bars/

**Yield:** 16 servings
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes
**Cook Time:** 30-40 minutes
**Total Time:** 2 hours

### Ingredients

**For the Crust & Cheesecake:**
- 6 sheets phyllo dough, defrosted
- 1/4 cup butter, melted
- 16 oz cream cheese, room temperature
- 1/3 cup sour cream
- 2 eggs, room temperature
- 2/3 cup granulated sugar
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 2 tablespoons honey

**For the Baklava Topping:**
- 1/2 cup honey
- 3/4 cup water
- 1 cup pistachios, finely chopped
- 1 cup walnuts, finely chopped
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1/4 teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 325°F; line 9-inch square pan with parchment paper
2. Layer 12 sheets of phyllo dough with melted butter between each layer
3. Blind bake phyllo for 15 minutes until golden brown
4. Beat softened cream cheese, sour cream, honey, and sugar until smooth; add vanilla and eggs one at a time
5. Pour cheesecake batter over crust; drop pan to release air bubbles
6. Bake 40 minutes on middle rack, rotating halfway (center will be wobbly)
7. Turn off oven; crack door slightly and cool cheesecake for 1 hour
8. Heat honey and water until reduced by half, then add nuts, lemon juice, and salt
9. Pour topping over cooled cheesecake; cover with foil and refrigerate overnight
10. Slice into 8 large or 16 smaller pieces when ready to serve

**Notes:** Best enjoyed on day 2. Stays fresh refrigerated for up to 7 days.

## 24. Golden Graham S'mores Bars

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/golden-graham-smores-bars/

**Yield:** 12 bars
**Prep Time:** 5 minutes
**Cook Time:** 0 minutes
**Total Time:** 30 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1/3 cup unsalted butter
- Pinch of salt
- 16 ounce bag of marshmallows
- 5 1/2 cups Golden Grahams
- 1/2 cup milk chocolate (roughly chopped Hershey's bars)
- 1 1/2 cups mini marshmallows (for folding into bars)

### Instructions
1. Grease a 9x9 pan with cooking spray and set aside.
2. In a large pot over medium heat, melt the butter.
3. Add regular marshmallows and salt; stir with a rubber spatula, scraping sides until melted. Remove from heat immediately.
4. In a large bowl, add Golden Grahams cereal and pour marshmallow mixture over it; fold together using a rubber spatula.
5. Stir in chocolate and mini marshmallows.
6. Spoon mixture into prepared pan, pressing down into an even layer.
7. Place in refrigerator to set for at least 20 minutes. Cut into 12 rectangles with a sharp knife.

**Notes:** If batter is especially sticky, spray rubber spatula with cooking spray before pressing bars into pan.
