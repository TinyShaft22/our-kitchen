## Recipe 19: Key Lime Pie Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/key-lime-pie-cookies/
**Yield:** 10 cookies
**Prep Time:** 40 minutes | **Cook Time:** 14 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 tablespoon fresh key lime zest
- 1 tablespoon fresh lime juice
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs

### Key Lime Pie Filling Ingredients
- 4 ounces cream cheese, room temperature
- 2 tablespoons fresh key lime juice
- 2 tablespoons sweetened condensed milk
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F; line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and both sugars until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
3. Add egg, vanilla, lime juice, and lime zest; beat until smooth (about 1 minute).
4. Mix in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt on low speed until combined; add graham cracker crumbs.
5. Scoop dough using a 1.5-ounce cookie scoop; create indentations in centers with thumb.
6. Bake 12-14 minutes until edges are firm; reshape indentations while hot. Cool completely.
7. Combine all filling ingredients; beat until smooth.
8. Spoon filling into cookie centers; chill at least 1 hour before serving.

## Recipe 20: Strawberry Shortcake Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/strawberry-shortcake-cookies/
**Yield:** 12 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15 minutes

### Crumble Topping Ingredients
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 2 tablespoons light brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar
- 1/3 cup all-purpose flour
- Pinch of salt

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg, at room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 6 strawberries, washed, hulled and sliced
- 1/4 cup strawberry preserves

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Mix flour, brown sugar, granulated sugar, salt, and melted butter until clumpy; set aside.
3. Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
4. Add egg and vanilla; beat until smooth (about 1 minute).
5. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt; mix on low until just combined.
6. Fold in sliced strawberries, then gently fold in preserves without overmixing.
7. Scoop dough onto baking sheet with 2-inch spacing; top each with 1 teaspoon crumble.
8. Bake 12-15 minutes until set around edges but soft in center; cool 5 minutes before transferring to rack.

## Recipe 21: Soft and Chewy Marble Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/soft-and-chewy-marble-cookies/
**Yield:** 10 cookies
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes | **Cook Time:** 13 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk, at room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/2 cup + 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons Dutch-process cocoa powder
- 1 cup chocolate chips

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In stand mixer with paddle attachment, combine butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar. Beat on medium-high speed until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Scrape bowl sides with spatula, add egg and vanilla extract. Beat until smooth and combined.
4. Add 1 1/2 cups flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Mix until just combined with no flour streaks remaining.
5. Divide dough in half. To first bowl, add remaining 3 tablespoons flour and 1/2 cup chocolate chips. To second bowl, add 3 tablespoons cocoa powder and 1/2 cup chocolate chips. Stir each to combine.
6. Using 0.5-ounce cookie scoop, alternate stacking four dough balls (vanilla, chocolate, vanilla, chocolate). Press together and roll into balls. Repeat with remaining dough.
7. Place balls 2 inches apart on prepared cookie sheet. Bake at 350°F for 12-14 minutes until edges are golden brown and centers have puffed but remain slightly underdone.
8. Cool for a few minutes on baking sheet, then transfer to wire rack to cool completely.

## Recipe 22: Tiramisu Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/tiramisu-cookies/
**Yield:** 12 cookies
**Prep Time:** 40 minutes | **Cook Time:** 13-14 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/4 cup espresso powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Mascarpone Frosting Ingredients
- 1/3 cup heavy cream
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 8 ounces mascarpone cheese
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt
- Dutch-process cocoa powder for dusting

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
3. Add egg and vanilla extract; beat until combined.
4. Add flour, espresso powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt on low speed until just combined.
5. Using a 1.5-ounce cookie scoop, place dough on baking sheet, spacing 2 inches apart.
6. Bake 13-14 minutes until edges are set but centers remain slightly gooey.
7. Cool completely on cooling rack.
8. Beat heavy cream and powdered sugar until combined (about 20 seconds). Add mascarpone, vanilla, and salt; beat only until combined (about 30 seconds). Pipe onto cooled cookies.
9. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

## Recipe 23: Funfetti Crinkle Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/funfetti-crinkle-cookies/
**Yield:** 16 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
- 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 1/4 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup rainbow sprinkles

### Coating Ingredients
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 1/3 cup powdered sugar

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F; line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. In stand mixer with paddle attachment, combine butter and granulated sugar. Beat on medium-to-high speed until light, fluffy, and pale (about 2 minutes).
3. Scrape bowl sides; add eggs, vanilla, and almond extract. Beat until smooth and combined.
4. Add flour, baking powder, and salt. Stir on low speed until combined with no flour streaks remaining. Don't overmix.
5. Fold in rainbow sprinkles until evenly distributed.
6. Fill two small bowls with granulated sugar and powdered sugar separately. Using a 1.5-ounce cookie scoop, roll dough balls first in granulated sugar, then in powdered sugar until fully coated.
7. Place cookies 2 inches apart on prepared sheet. Bake 10-12 minutes until edges are set and centers have puffed but remain gooey. Cool completely before serving.

## Recipe 24: Red Velvet Cheesecake Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/red-velvet-cheesecake-cookies/
**Yield:** 16 cookies
**Prep Time:** 1 hour | **Cook Time:** 15-16 minutes

### Cheesecake Center Ingredients
- 6 ounces cream cheese, at room temperature
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk, at room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon red gel food coloring
- 1/3 cup Dutch-process cocoa powder
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar (optional for rolling)

### Instructions
1. Mix softened cream cheese, powdered sugar, flour, vanilla, and salt until smooth. Pipe into discs on parchment paper and freeze for at least 1 hour.
2. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
3. Cream butter and sugars until light and fluffy. Add egg, yolk, and vanilla. Mix in red food coloring.
4. Add cocoa powder, flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix until combined.
5. Scoop dough, flatten slightly, place frozen cheesecake center on top, and seal edges.
6. Roll in sugar if desired. Place on prepared sheet 2 inches apart.
7. Bake 14-16 minutes until puffed and crinkled. Cool slightly, then transfer to cooling rack.

## Recipe 25: Chocolate Covered Strawberry Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/chocolate-covered-strawberry-cookies/
**Yield:** 12 cookies
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup Dutch-process cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup freeze-dried strawberries
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

### Chocolate Coating Ingredients
- 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- 1 teaspoon coconut oil

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a stand mixer with paddle attachment, beat butter and brown sugar on medium-high speed until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
3. Scrape bowl sides, then add egg and vanilla. Beat on medium-high speed until light and pale (about 2 minutes).
4. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Beat on low speed until combined with no flour streaks (about 30 seconds). Fold in freeze-dried strawberries and chocolate chips.
5. Using a 1.5-ounce cookie scoop, portion dough onto prepared sheets, spacing 2 inches apart. Bake 10-12 minutes until cookies puff and are firm at edges but gooey in center.
6. Cool on baking sheets for 15 minutes.
7. Microwave chocolate chips and coconut oil in 30-second increments, stirring between each, until melted.
8. Dip cookie bottoms in chocolate, scrape excess, and place on cool parchment. Drizzle tops if desired. Allow 30 minutes for chocolate to fully set.

## Recipe 26: Maple Pecan Crumble Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/maple-pecan-crumble-cookies/
**Yield:** 14 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12-15 minutes

### Cinnamon Sugar Swirl Ingredients
- 1/4 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 3/4 cup light brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons pure maple syrup
- 1 egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 teaspoon maple extract
- 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

### Maple Pecan Crumble Topping Ingredients
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons light brown sugar, packed
- 1 teaspoon pure maple syrup
- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons chopped pecans
- Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F and line a cookie sheet with parchment paper.
2. Mix brown sugar and cinnamon together in a small bowl; set aside.
3. In a stand mixer with paddle attachment, beat butter and brown sugar until light and airy (about 2 minutes).
4. Scrape down bowl sides; add maple syrup, egg, vanilla, and maple extract. Beat until smooth and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
5. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix on low speed, increasing to high until no flour streaks remain; do not overmix.
6. Fold in the cinnamon-sugar mixture one or two times to create visible swirls; avoid overmixing.
7. In a separate bowl, combine all streusel topping ingredients; mix with a fork until crumbly.
8. Scoop dough using a 1.5-ounce cookie scoop onto prepared baking sheet. Flatten tops slightly and top each with a heaping spoonful of streusel, pressing firmly.
9. Bake 12-15 minutes until edges are set but centers remain slightly doughy. Cool completely before serving.

## Recipe 27: Chewy Chocolate Gingerbread Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/chewy-chocolate-gingerbread-cookies/
**Yield:** 14 cookies
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12 minutes

### Ingredients
- 3/4 cup unsalted butter
- 3/4 cup brown sugar
- 1/3 cup molasses
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 1/2 cup Dutch-process cocoa powder
- 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 2 teaspoons ground ginger
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup cane sugar (for rolling)

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F; line two cookie sheets with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and sugar together until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
3. Mix in molasses and vanilla extract until combined.
4. Add egg and beat well.
5. In a separate bowl, combine cocoa, flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, ginger, cloves, nutmeg, and salt.
6. Add dry ingredients to wet mixture; beat until combined with no flour streaks remaining.
7. Using a 1.5-ounce cookie scoop, portion cookies. Roll in cane sugar if desired; place 2 inches apart on prepared sheets.
8. Bake 12 minutes until puffed and crinkled with gooey centers. Cool completely before transferring to wire rack.
