## Recipe 10: Peppermint Crinkle Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/peppermint-crinkle-cookies-but-like-actually-brookies/
**Yield:** 16 cookies
**Prep Time:** 20 minutes | **Cook Time:** 10 minutes

### Coating Ingredients
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 2/3 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/3 cup granulated sugar
- 2 large eggs, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 3/4 teaspoon peppermint extract
- 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 cup Dutch processed cocoa powder
- 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper. Separate sugars into bowls.
2. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Add eggs, vanilla, and peppermint extract. Beat until smooth and fluffy.
4. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Beat on low until combined.
5. Scoop 1.5-ounce dough balls. Roll in granulated sugar first, then powdered sugar.
6. Bake 9-10 minutes until cracked and firm at edges but soft in center.

## Recipe 11: Gingerbread Iced Oatmeal Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/gingerbread-iced-oatmeal-cookies/
**Yield:** 15 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 9-10 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
- 2/3 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 2 tablespoons molasses
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 cup old fashioned oats
- 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 1/2 teaspoons ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground cloves
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Icing Ingredients
- 1 1/3 cups powdered sugar
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Combine melted butter, sugars, and molasses. Add egg, egg yolk, and vanilla; mix well.
2. Fold in flour, oats, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, cloves, and salt. Let dough rest 10 minutes.
3. Preheat oven to 350°F. Scoop 1.5-ounce dough balls, space 2 inches apart.
4. Bake 9-10 minutes until puffed and edges are light golden.
5. Whisk icing ingredients. Spoon over warm cookies and spread to edges. Let set.

## Recipe 12: Soft Gingerbread Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/soft-gingerbread-cookies/
**Yield:** 20 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 8-9 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup shortening (or butter)
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1 cup molasses
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 2 teaspoons ground ginger
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Royal Icing Ingredients
- 1 egg white
- 1 1/4 cups powdered sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Combine shortening, sugar, and molasses in saucepan over medium-low heat. Stir until melted. Cool slightly.
2. Whisk egg and vanilla into cooled mixture.
3. Fold in flour, baking soda, ginger, cinnamon, and salt. Wrap dough and chill at least 2 hours.
4. Preheat oven to 350°F. Roll dough to 1/4-inch thickness. Cut shapes, place 2 inches apart.
5. Bake 8-9 minutes until edges are lightly browned. Cool completely.
6. Whisk icing ingredients. Pipe onto cooled cookies.

## Recipe 13: Soft and Chewy Buckeye Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/soft-and-chewy-buckeye-cookies/
**Yield:** 10 cookies
**Prep Time:** 45 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg, at room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/3 cup Dutch-processed cocoa powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Peanut Butter Filling Ingredients
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 2/3 cup powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Chocolate Topping Ingredients
- 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
- 1/4 cup heavy cream

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and brown sugar until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Add egg and vanilla; beat until fluffy and pale.
4. Add flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, and salt. Beat on low until combined.
5. Scoop 2-ounce dough balls, space 2 inches apart. Bake 11-12 minutes. Press cookie scoop into centers to create indentations. Cool completely.
6. Mix peanut butter filling ingredients. Scoop onto cooled cookies.
7. Heat cream, pour over chocolate chips. Stir until smooth. Top each cookie with ganache. Let set 30 minutes.

## Recipe 14: White Chocolate Pistachio Cranberry Slice and Bake Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/white-chocolate-pistachio-cranberry-slice-and-bake-cookies/
**Yield:** 50 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 10 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 3/4 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg yolk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup unsalted pistachios, finely chopped, divided
- 1/2 cup dried cranberries, roughly chopped
- 4 ounces white chocolate, finely chopped

### Optional White Chocolate Coating
- 4 ounces white chocolate, finely chopped
- 1 teaspoon coconut oil

### Instructions
1. Beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy (about 2 minutes).
2. Add egg yolk, vanilla, and almond extract. Beat until smooth.
3. Add flour and salt, beat on low until combined. Fold in 1/2 cup pistachios, cranberries, and chocolate.
4. Divide dough in half. Roll each into 12-inch log, 1-inch diameter. Roll in remaining pistachios. Wrap and refrigerate 2 hours.
5. Preheat oven to 350°F. Cut logs into 1/4-inch rounds. Bake 10 minutes until golden. Cool completely.
6. For coating: melt white chocolate with coconut oil. Dip or drizzle cookies.

## Recipe 15: Pumpkin Pie Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/pumpkin-pie-cookies/
**Yield:** 14 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 3/4 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/4 cups graham cracker crumbs

### Pumpkin Filling Ingredients
- 1/3 cup pumpkin puree
- 3 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg
- 1 tablespoon evaporated milk
- 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and sugars until combined. Add egg, milk, and vanilla.
3. Add flour, graham cracker crumbs, baking powder, and salt. Beat until combined.
4. Scoop 1.5-ounce dough balls. Flatten and press thumb into center. Bake 5 minutes.
5. Whisk pumpkin filling ingredients together.
6. Remove cookies after 5 minutes. Reshape center divot. Spoon 2 teaspoons filling into each center.
7. Bake 6-8 more minutes until golden and filling sets. Top with whipped cream if desired.

## Recipe 16: Dirty Chai Snickerdoodles

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/dirty-chai-snickerdoodles/
**Yield:** 16 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12-13 minutes

### Dirty Chai Sugar Ingredients
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder
- 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
- Crack of fresh black pepper

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/3 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk, room temperature
- 1 tablespoon plain Greek yogurt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
- 1 teaspoon ground ginger
- 1 teaspoon ground cardamom
- 1/2 teaspoon espresso powder
- 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
- Crack of fresh black pepper
- 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Mix dirty chai sugar ingredients in small bowl.
3. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy, about 2 minutes.
4. Add egg, egg yolk, Greek yogurt, and vanilla. Beat until fluffy and pale.
5. Add flour, spices, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt. Beat until combined.
6. Scoop 2-ounce dough balls. Roll in dirty chai sugar. Space 3 inches apart.
7. Bake 12-13 minutes until crackled and soft in center.

## Recipe 17: Homemade Nutter Butters

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/homemade-nutter-butters/
**Yield:** 16 sandwich cookies
**Prep Time:** 45 minutes | **Cook Time:** 10 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 3/4 cup creamy peanut butter
- 1/4 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 2 tablespoons cornstarch
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Filling Ingredients
- 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
- 2/3 cup powdered sugar
- 3-4 tablespoons heavy cream
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Beat peanut butter, butter, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add egg and vanilla.
3. Add flour, cornstarch, baking soda, and salt. Beat until combined.
4. Scoop 1-ounce portions into oval discs. Pinch middle to resemble peanut shape. Make crosshatches with fork.
5. Bake 9-10 minutes until puffed and set. Cool completely.
6. Make filling: beat peanut butter, powdered sugar, cream, and vanilla until smooth.
7. Match cookies into pairs. Pipe filling onto one half, sandwich together.

## Recipe 18: Apple Fritter Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/apple-fritter-cookies/
**Yield:** 16 cookies
**Prep Time:** 45 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15 minutes

### Apple Ingredients
- 1 large apple, peeled, cored and diced (Granny Smith recommended)
- 2 tablespoons packed light brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 2/3 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg, at room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 2/3 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

### Maple Glaze Ingredients
- 1 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon pure maple syrup
- 2 tablespoons milk
- Heavy pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Toss diced apple with brown sugar and cinnamon.
3. Beat butter and brown sugar until fluffy, about 2 minutes. Add egg and vanilla.
4. Add flour, baking powder, spices, and salt. Beat until combined. Fold in apple mixture.
5. Scoop 2-ounce dough balls, space 2 inches apart.
6. Bake 12-15 minutes until puffed and set at edges.
7. Whisk glaze ingredients together. Spoon over hot cookies. Let set 10 minutes.
