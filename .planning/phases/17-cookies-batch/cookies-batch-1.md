## Recipe 1: Soft and Chewy Christmas Sprinkle Sugar Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/soft-and-chewy-christmas-sprinkle-sugar-cookies/
**Yield:** 15 cookies
**Prep Time:** 15 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12 minutes

### Ingredients
- 14 tablespoons unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
- 2 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup holiday sprinkles

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. In a stand mixer with paddle attachment, combine butter and sugars. Beat on low until combined, then increase to medium-high and beat until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Scrape bowl sides, add egg, egg yolk, vanilla, and almond extract. Beat on medium-high until fluffy and lightened, about 2 minutes.
4. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Beat on low until just combined. Fold in sprinkles gently.
5. Use a 1.5 ounce cookie scoop to portion dough. Place on prepared sheets, spacing 2 inches apart.
6. Bake 11-12 minutes until edges are set but centers remain soft. Cool slightly before serving.

## Recipe 2: Apple Crisp Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/apple-crisp-cookies/
**Yield:** 10 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15-16 minutes

### Crisp Topping Ingredients
- 3 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
- 3 tablespoons light brown sugar
- 4 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- 3 tablespoons old fashioned oats
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 2/3 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 large apple, peeled, cored, and diced
- 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
- 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger

### Optional Vanilla Icing
- 1/2 cup powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon milk
- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Pinch of salt

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Make crisp topping: combine melted butter, brown sugar, flour, oats, and cinnamon until mixture clumps.
3. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy, about 2 minutes. Add egg and vanilla, beat 1 minute.
4. Add flour, baking soda, and salt. Beat on low until combined.
5. Toss apple pieces with cinnamon, nutmeg, and ginger. Fold into dough.
6. Scoop dough with 2-ounce scoop. Press crisp topping over each ball.
7. Bake 15-16 minutes until puffed and set at edges. Cool 5 minutes.
8. Whisk icing ingredients and drizzle over cookies if desired.

## Recipe 3: Brown Butter Walnut Chocolate Chip Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/brown-butter-walnut-chocolate-chip-cookies/
**Yield:** 15 cookies
**Prep Time:** 45 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12-13 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1 cup walnuts, roughly chopped
- 3/4 cup (168g) unsalted butter
- 1 cup (200g) packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk, room temperature
- 1 tablespoon pure vanilla extract
- 1 3/4 cup (220g) all-purpose flour
- 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 3/4 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
- Flaky salt for topping

### Instructions
1. Toast walnuts at 375°F for 5-7 minutes until golden.
2. Brown butter in saucepan over medium-low heat until golden with brown bits, 5-7 minutes. Cool slightly.
3. Combine brown butter and sugars. Add egg, egg yolk, and vanilla; mix well.
4. Fold in flour, baking soda, and salt until no streaks remain.
5. Fold in chocolate chips and walnuts. Cover and refrigerate 30 minutes.
6. Scoop into 1.5-ounce balls. Refrigerate at least 30 more minutes.
7. Preheat oven to 350°F. Bake 12-13 minutes until edges are golden. Top with flaky salt.

## Recipe 4: Single Serve Funfetti Cookie

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/single-serve-funfetti-cookie/
**Yield:** 2 cookies
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12-13 minutes

### Ingredients
- 2 tablespoons (28g) unsalted butter, room temperature
- 2 tablespoons (26g) packed light brown sugar
- 1 tablespoon (13g) granulated sugar
- 1 large egg yolk
- Splash vanilla extract
- Splash almond extract
- 5 tablespoons (39g) all-purpose flour
- 1/8 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/8 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/8 teaspoon salt
- 2 tablespoons white chocolate, chopped
- 1 tablespoon rainbow sprinkles

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Mix butter and sugars until combined.
3. Add egg yolk, vanilla, and almond extract; mix well.
4. Stir in flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt. Fold in white chocolate and sprinkles.
5. Divide dough in half and roll into balls. Place 3 inches apart.
6. Bake 12-13 minutes until edges are set but center remains soft.

## Recipe 5: Banana Cream Pie Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/banana-cream-pie-cookies/
**Yield:** 14 cookies
**Prep Time:** 45 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12 minutes

### Cookie Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1/2 cup light brown sugar, packed
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 cups Nilla wafer crumbs

### Topping Ingredients
- 1 packet (3.4 oz) vanilla instant pudding mix
- 2/3 cup whole milk
- Pinch of salt
- 1 cup heavy whipping cream
- 1 large banana, sliced into rounds

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Add egg and vanilla; beat until smooth.
4. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Mix in Nilla wafer crumbs.
5. Scoop 1.5-ounce dough balls, space 2 inches apart.
6. Bake 12 minutes until edges are set but centers soft. Cool completely.
7. Whisk pudding mix with milk and salt. Refrigerate until thickened.
8. Whip heavy cream to medium peaks. Fold in pudding.
9. Top cookies with banana slices and pipe cream mixture on top.

## Recipe 6: Neapolitan Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/neapolitan-cookies/
**Yield:** 12 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar
- 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1 1/3 cups + 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/3 cup freeze-dried strawberries, finely ground
- 1 tablespoon Dutch-process cocoa powder
- 1/2 cup granulated sugar (for coating)

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Add egg and vanilla; beat until smooth.
4. Add 1 1/3 cups flour, baking soda, baking powder, and salt; mix until combined.
5. Divide dough into thirds. Add 1 tbsp flour to first, freeze-dried strawberries to second, cocoa powder to third.
6. Take one ball from each flavor, press together and roll until combined.
7. Roll in granulated sugar. Place 2 inches apart.
8. Bake 12-14 minutes until edges are golden. Cool before serving.

## Recipe 7: Lemon Blueberry White Chocolate Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/lemon-blueberry-cookies/
**Yield:** 12 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 15 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
- 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg, room temperature
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/2 teaspoon lemon extract
- 1 tablespoon lemon zest
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
- 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 cup frozen blueberries
- 1/4 cup blueberry preserves
- 4 ounces white chocolate, roughly chopped

### Instructions
1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper.
2. Beat butter and sugars until fluffy, about 2 minutes.
3. Add egg, vanilla, lemon extract, and lemon zest. Beat until smooth.
4. Add flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt. Beat on low until combined.
5. Fold in frozen blueberries and white chocolate. Drop blueberry preserves in spoonfuls and fold gently for ripple effect.
6. Scoop 1.5-ounce dough balls, place 2 inches apart.
7. Bake 12-15 minutes until puffed and set around edges. Garnish with lemon zest.

## Recipe 8: Salted Pistachio Chocolate Chunk Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/salted-pistachio-chocolate-chunk-cookies/
**Yield:** 14 cookies
**Prep Time:** 30 minutes | **Cook Time:** 12-14 minutes

### Ingredients
- 3/4 cup (100g) roasted pistachios, finely ground
- 3/4 cup (170g) unsalted butter, melted
- 3/4 cup (150g) packed light brown sugar
- 1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar
- 1 large egg + 1 large egg yolk, room temperature
- 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon almond extract (optional)
- 1 3/4 cups (220g) all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 2.5 ounces white chocolate, roughly chopped
- 3 ounces dark chocolate, roughly chopped
- Flaky salt for topping

### Instructions
1. Pulse pistachios in food processor until finely ground (don't over-blend).
2. Combine melted butter and sugars. Add egg, egg yolk, vanilla, and almond extract; mix well.
3. Add ground pistachios, flour, baking soda, and salt. Fold until combined.
4. Fold in white and dark chocolate. Refrigerate at least 30 minutes.
5. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheet with parchment paper.
6. Scoop 2-ounce dough balls, space 2 inches apart.
7. Bake 12-14 minutes until edges are golden and centers gooey. Top with flaky salt.

## Recipe 9: The BEST Sugar Cookies

**URL:** https://bromabakery.com/sugar-cookies/
**Yield:** 20 medium cookies
**Prep Time:** 10 minutes | **Cook Time:** 10 minutes

### Ingredients
- 1/2 cup unsalted butter, at room temperature
- 1 cup granulated sugar
- 1 large egg, at room temperature
- 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
- 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
- 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Instructions
1. Beat butter and sugar until just combined, about 45 seconds. Add egg, vanilla, and almond extract; beat 1 minute.
2. Add flour and salt, mixing until combined. Form into flat disk, wrap in plastic. Refrigerate at least 1 hour.
3. Preheat oven to 350°F. Line baking sheets with parchment paper.
4. Roll dough to 1/4 inch thickness on floured surface. Cut shapes, place on sheets.
5. Sprinkle with colored sugar if desired. Bake 8-12 minutes until edges are just golden.
6. Cool completely before frosting or serve plain.
